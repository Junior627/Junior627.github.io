Copy n' Haste

A Quick Sketch-y Videogame

Overview

Copy n' Haste is a videogame where you must draw counterfeit pictures of famous paintings. The more accurate your drawing, the more money you get as a reward!

Role: Director, Programmer
Duration: April 2025 (2 Weeks)
Medium: Digital Video Game (made in Unity / C#)
Team Size: 3
